Built on top of the [Chiri](https://github.com/the3ash/astro-chiri) theme, this site uses Astro content collections for blog posts and projects. Pages are statically generated at build time, so they load fast with no client-side framework overhead.

## Highlights

- Content collections with typed frontmatter
- Minimal, typography-focused design
- RSS and Atom feeds out of the box
